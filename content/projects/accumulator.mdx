## What I did
- Defined design criteria with EE team; designed for strict FSAE rules and tight packaging.
- Thermal models showed passive cooling sufficient; eliminated active cooling complexity.
- Custom PCB for voltage/temperature logging; thermal relief features on busbars.

## Results
- Built and integrated in ~2 weeks.
- Ran reliably throughout the testing season.
