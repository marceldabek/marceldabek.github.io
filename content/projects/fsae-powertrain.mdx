## What I did
- Researched 40+ team designs; selected core components (Emrax motor + Drexler differential).
- Free-body diagrams for 5 major parts; ANSYS static structural + topology optimization.
- Vibration analysis to move natural frequency above motor operating range.

## Results
- ≈30% powertrain mass reduction vs prior iteration.
- Manufactured and tested for ~6 months without failures.
